About My idol

Lu Han (born April 20, 1990) is a Chinese singer and actor. He was a member, known as Luhan of the South Korean-Chinese boy group EXO and its sub-group EXO-M, before leaving the group in October 2014.[1] That year, he was ranked the 6th most popular entertainment star in China by China National Radio.[2] In 2017, Lu Han was listed as the second highest-paid celebrity in the Forbes China Celebrity 100 list, behind only Fan Bingbing.[3]

Lu Han released his solo debut album, Reloaded in 2015; and has starred in several box office hits such as 20 Once Again (2015), The Witness (2015) and Time Raiders (2016). In 2017, he starred in his first television series, Fighter of the Destiny.
